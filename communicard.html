Welcome to CommuniCard!

Cards for people with communication disabilities

This card is to help YOU communicate more easily.
You can create it here for free.

CommuniCard is an interactive platform that creates a personalized card introducing the person with a communication disability and explaining how to better communicate with them. We believe that assisted conversation promotes participation.

Let’s start!
In the next stages we will ask you to fill in the details for your personalized card.

Before we start – note to users:

If you are creating the card for yourself – press continue!

If you are assisting someone else make their card please read the following note:

You have an important role in enabling the person you are assisting control the content and design of their card.
- We suggest presenting the options one by one and giving sufficient reaction time, to support the person in making their choice.
- Once the preview of the card is ready, present it to the person and allow them to request changes before finalizing the card.
